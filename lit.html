This is an unprocessed dump (raw HTML) of close to a million threads from Literotica Forums, crawled from the archives. Please feel free to use it for research purposes. The zip is 5.2GB compressed, and 25GB uncompressed.

Please let me know if you used the data in a published paper, then I'll add the paper to the following table.

A processed dataset of Literotica stories is available from Mark Allen Thornton. For details, please see his blog entries (link 1, link 2).




Open Research Ideas

I'm documenting some thoughts and research ideas here.
Feel free to use these thoughts in your research.

If you're writing a paper on any of these ideas, I don't want co-authorship.
Instead, a simple acknowledgement to this webpage will suffice.

Right now, I'm not open to collaboration, so please feel free to work with anyone you like.
However, if you want to contribute to these thoughts, and/or have some comments, and/or would like to add/alter/modify ideas, please email me.
